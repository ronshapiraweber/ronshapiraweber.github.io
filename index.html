About

I am a Ph.D. Candidate at Ben Gurion University at the Vision, Inference, and Learning (VIL) under the supervision of Dr. Oren Freifeld at the Computer Science Dept. My research areas are time series analysis and computer vision, with applications to time series alignment and image registration.

I did my master's in Cognitive Science at Ben Gurion University as a part of VIL group, under the supervision of Dr. Oren Freifeld at the Computer Science Dept. and of the Computational Psychiatry Lab at the Dept. of Brain and Cognitive Science, under the supervision of Dr. Oren Shriki.

Between 2019-2021 I took a hiatus from academia and worked as an algorithm researcher in BeyondMinds.
